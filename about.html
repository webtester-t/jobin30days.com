Meet Your Mentor

Learn from an industry expert with over 2 decades of experience

Priyojeet Dey

Mentor & a Leader with 27+ Years Experience

Priyojeet Dey is a seasoned professional and career mentor with over 27 years of experience spanning project management, 24×7 service delivery, client engagement, business development, and team leadership. He combines deep operational discipline with strategic foresight, helping professionals accelerate their career growth, refine their job search outcomes, and elevate personal effectiveness.

As the Founder and Chief Mentor of JOB IN 30 DAYS™, Priyojeet is dedicated to empowering job seekers to significantly improve their chances of securing meaningful opportunities through structured career guidance, personalised coaching, and actionable job search strategies. He leads with authenticity, prioritising high-quality results, meticulous planning, and outcome-oriented execution.



Professional Expertise

  • End-to-End Career Mentorship: Priyojeet guides individuals through every stage of the job search, from crafting compelling CVs and optimising LinkedIn profiles to developing interview confidence and professional brand visibility. His approach is practical, personalised, and rooted in decades of professional insight.
  • Leadership & Team Development: Known for his ability to lead diverse teams, Priyojeet balances perfection with progress—ensuring that goals are met without compromising quality. He is an attentive listener, effective communicator, and a mentor who encourages mentees to stretch beyond their comfort zones for meaningful growth.
  • Client & Stakeholder Management: With a strong client focus, he maintains robust relationships, helping professionals refine their interpersonal skills and navigational savvy during career transitions and workplace challenges.

Mentorship Philosophy
Priyojeet believes that professional success is not just about accumulating skills but about strategic application, resilience, and continuous learning. He emphasises clarity of purpose, disciplined execution, and the cultivation of soft skills—such as communication, prioritisation, and leadership—that distinguish successful professionals in today’s competitive job market.
Under his mentorship at JOB IN 30 DAYS™, individuals are equipped with a clear roadmap to navigate job search complexities, build confidence, and achieve measurable career outcomes within defined timelines.

Impact & Reach
Priyojeet’s thought leadership extends well beyond one-on-one coaching. He actively shares insights on evolving job market trends, future skill demands, and career development tactics on professional platforms, fostering a community of lifelong learners and high-achieving professionals.
